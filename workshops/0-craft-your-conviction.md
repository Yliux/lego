### Step 4. Prototype and sketching

**Start sketching first variations to solve the problem statement.**

It should be done in black and white and in a simple way.

The required elements are:

* Buttons
* Fields
* Some identifying text (title,price, etc..)
* Placeholder for text, images, icons, logo
* Drop-down menu
* Including several variations
* Filtering and sorting
* Saving 

You can exclude elements such as color, typography, real icons, logo and pictures.

My recommendation is to

* **Draw layout structure with basic shapes** and lines to represent the layout structure of your wireframe. Start with simple rectangles to represent content. 

* **Add step by step basic content elements** to your wireframe, such as text boxes, buttons, images, or icons. Use simple shapes or placeholder text to represent these elements.

* **Stick to a black-and-white (remember paper and pen) color scheme** for your low-fidelity wireframe. This helps focus on the layout and structure without getting distracted by visual design elements. 

* **Avoid excessive and unnecessary details**. The purpose is to convey the layout, structure, and content hierarchy.
